Project Impact

This Digital Twin Platform represents a significant step forward in heritage site management across South Asia. By combining real-time AI monitoring with predictive analytics, the system has the potential to reduce overcrowding incidents by an estimated 60%, improve visitor experience scores, and enable data-driven decisions for site management authorities.

Beyond Sigiriya, the modular architecture of this platform is designed for scalability — enabling deployment at other UNESCO World Heritage Sites across Sri Lanka and potentially throughout the region, including sites like Polonnaruwa, Dambulla, and Kandy.
