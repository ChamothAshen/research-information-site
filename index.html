About Sigiriya Rock Fortress

Sigiriya is a UNESCO World Heritage Site and one of Sri Lanka's most popular tourist destinations, attracting hundreds of thousands of visitors annually. This Digital Twin platform aims to revolutionize how this priceless heritage site is managed, ensuring safety, efficiency, and enhanced visitor experience through cutting-edge AI technology.
